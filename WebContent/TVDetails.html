What are the different TV resolution types?
If you are looking for TVs for business-related purposes, WXGA resolution TVs are the ideal choice. These TVs come with a screen resolution of approximately 1280x800, and are ideal for watching DVDs. For regular home-viewing, you can choose from HD, HD Ready, Full HD TV, and 4K Ultra HD televisions. The picture-quality of a TV depends on its pixel quantity. The more pixels it has, the better its picture-clarity will be.
